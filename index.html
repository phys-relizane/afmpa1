Welcome to AFMPA1

The AFMPA1 conference aims to provide a scientific forum for researchers, academics, engineers, and industry professionals working in the fields of advanced functional materials and physics applications. Through interdisciplinary exchange and collaboration, we aim to accelerate the transfer of scientific advances into practical solutions.
