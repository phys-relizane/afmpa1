Conference Objectives

The AFMPA1 conference aims to provide a scientific forum for researchers, academics, engineers, and industry professionals working in the fields of advanced functional materials and physics applications.

The conference seeks to:

  • Promote the development of innovative materials for sustainable energy generation, storage, and conversion.
  • Advance knowledge in nanostructured and multifunctional materials through the exploration of their physical properties, design, and technological applications.
  • Encourage research on smart materials and intelligent systems for sensing, actuation, monitoring, and automation.
  • Foster the integration of artificial intelligence, computational modeling, and data-driven approaches in materials discovery and optimization.

"Through interdisciplinary exchange and collaboration, AFMPA1 aims to accelerate the transfer of scientific advances into practical solutions that support sustainability, energy efficiency, technological innovation, and industrial development."
