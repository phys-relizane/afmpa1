Registration Guidelines

Abstract Guidelines

Authors should prepare their abstract using one of the official AFMPA1 templates before submission.

  • Use a clear title, author names, affiliations, and contact email.
  • Keep the abstract concise and focused on objectives, methods, results, and conclusions.
  • Upload the completed abstract file through the official Google Form.
